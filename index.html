// Security & Trust

A network built on
verified trust.

Every Keeper is verified. Every parcel is insured. Every transaction is auditable. Neary doesn't rely on goodwill — it engineers it.

🪪

Tiered ID Verification

Level 1: Photo ID + proof of address for standard parcels (<£50). Level 2: Basic DBS check for Gold Keepers accepting high-value items.

🛡️

Automatic Insurance — Up to £200

Every parcel held by a Keeper is insured from the moment it's scanned in — at zero cost to the shopper or Keeper.

📷

AI Condition Check-In

Computer Vision scans photos of arriving parcels for damage or tampering. Instant courier alert before the driver even leaves.

🔐

One-Time QR Handover

Parcels are only released when the Keeper scans the shopper's unique collection QR code. No QR = no handover. No exceptions.

⭐

Live Rating System

Keepers must maintain a 4.5+ star rating to stay active. Top performers earn the Gold Keeper badge and higher earnings.

🤖

AI Fraud Detection

Anomaly detection monitors Keeper behaviour in real time. Unusual patterns trigger instant identity re-verification before any handover.
