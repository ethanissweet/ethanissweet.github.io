// Security & Trust

A network built on
verified trust.

Every Keeper is verified. Every parcel is insured. Every transaction is auditable. Neary doesn't rely on goodwill — it engineers it.

🪪

Tiered ID Verification

Level 1: Photo ID + proof of address for standard parcels (<£50). Level 2: Basic DBS check for Gold Keepers accepting high-value items (laptops, jewelry).

🛡️

Automatic Insurance Cover

Every parcel held by a Keeper is insured up to £200 from the moment it's scanned — at zero cost to the shopper or Keeper. Funded from Neary's platform margin.

📷

Computer Vision Check-In

AI scans photos of arriving parcels for damage, tampering, or condition issues. Instant courier alert if anything is flagged — before the driver leaves.

🔐

One-Time QR Handover

Parcels are only released when the Keeper scans the shopper's unique collection QR code. No QR, no handover. No exceptions.

⭐

Live Rating System

Keepers must maintain a 4.5+ star rating to stay active. Drop below, and automatic review is triggered. Top performers earn the Gold Keeper badge.

🤖

AI Fraud Detection

Anomaly detection monitors Keeper behavior in real-time. Unusual patterns (e.g., accepting high-value items after long inactivity) trigger instant identity verification.
